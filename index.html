Pakrypt

Encrypt and Store Sensitive Data on the Device You Trust

Encrypt and Store

Securely encrypt your sensitive data into a "pak" right in the browser. Your encrypted data is stored on your device in the browser's local storage and is secured with a passphrase. Keep track of passwords, notes, and other small files.

Export and Import

Easily export and import "paks", which can be shared via email or as plain text in a pinch.
